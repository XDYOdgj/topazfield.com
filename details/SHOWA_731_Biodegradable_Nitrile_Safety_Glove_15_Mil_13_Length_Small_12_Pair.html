SHOWA 731 Biodegradable Nitrile Safety Glove

Engineered for both performance and sustainability, the SHOWA 731 glove is a perfect choice for professionals who value safety without compromising the planet.

Constructed from biodegradable nitrile, these gloves are designed to break down naturally at the end of their lifecycle, making them an excellent option for eco-conscious users. The 15 mil thickness ensures reliable protection against a variety of chemicals, solvents, and acids, making them ideal for industrial and laboratory environments.

Featuring a built-in cotton liner, the SHOWA 731 offers enhanced comfort and improved dexterity, allowing for better grip in both dry and wet conditions. This makes them perfect for tasks that require precision, whether you're handling food, cleaning, or working with hazardous materials.

With a 13" length and small size design, these gloves provide full hand coverage and fit a wide range of hand sizes, ensuring a secure and comfortable fit. Their combination of durability, chemical resistance, and eco-friendly properties makes them an excellent choice for daily use in food service and light industrial settings.

Choose the SHOWA 731 for a reliable barrier against spills and harmful substances, and enjoy the peace of mind that comes with using gloves that are both safe and sustainable for your hands and the environment.
