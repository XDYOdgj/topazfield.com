Poeticcity Marble Granite Agate Triangle Scrub Sponges

These beautifully designed scrub sponges are made from soft and dense kapok cotton, offering a perfect blend of strength and gentleness for all your cleaning needs. Whether in the kitchen, bathroom, or around the home, they deliver reliable performance without harsh scrubbing. The unique black and grey marble pattern with silver and white lines adds an elegant aesthetic to your cleaning routine, making these sponges not just functional but also stylish.
