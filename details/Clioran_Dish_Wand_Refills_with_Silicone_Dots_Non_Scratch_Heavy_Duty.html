Clioran Dish Wand Refills

Smart cleaning begins with the perfect tool. Clioran dish wand refills are engineered with innovation to make your daily chores easier and more effective.

Why Choose Clioran Dish Wand Refills?

Our unique design eliminates the central hole, preventing soap from leaking and maximizing absorption. This ensures a richer lather, making every scrubbing session more efficient and powerful.

Constructed with premium materials and advanced molding techniques, these refills offer enhanced durability. The thicker sponge increases scrubbing strength by 20%, while the secure grip ensures a tight fit on your dish wand.

Perfect for everything from delicate surfaces to tough stains, these sponges are non-scratch and gentle on your kitchenware. Say goodbye to scratches and hello to spotless results with every use.

Versatile & Reusable

Whether you're tackling stubborn stains, baked-on grime, or everyday cleaning, Clioran’s refills are your reliable partner. With their two-layer scrubbing system—combining powerful rubber dots and absorbent cellulose—you can clean dishes, pots, pans, glassware, sinks, bathtubs, ovens, and even floors with ease.

These refills are easy to install and designed for long-term use, making them a cost-effective and eco-friendly solution for both home and professional kitchens. Durable, leak-proof, and built to last, they’re the ideal choice for anyone who values quality and performance.

Key Features

  • No more leaks: Design eliminates the central hole for better soap retention
  • Premium quality: Advanced molding ensures durability and secure fit
  • Wide compatibility: Fits most dish wands including HELIME models
  • Non-scratch surface: Silicone dots clean effectively without damaging surfaces
  • Two-layer scrubbing: Combines powerful rubber dots with absorbent cellulose for efficient cleaning
