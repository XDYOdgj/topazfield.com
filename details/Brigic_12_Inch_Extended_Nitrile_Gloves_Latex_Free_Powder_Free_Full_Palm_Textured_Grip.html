Brigic 12-inch Extended Nitrile Gloves

Designed to keep your hands protected in even the toughest settings, these 12-inch extended nitrile gloves feature long cuffs that fully cover your wrists and forearms. The powder-free construction ensures a clean, irritation-free fit, making them ideal for both daily and professional use without compromising comfort.
