Scotch-Brite Classic Heavy Duty Scrub Sponge

Perfect for tackling messy kitchen jobs, this high-density scrub sponge is designed to make cleaning easier and more efficient. Its robust construction ensures it lasts through tough cleaning sessions, making it a reliable choice for everyday use.

Why You'll Love This Sponge

With a coarse yet effective scrubbing surface, this sponge is ideal for removing stubborn grease, grime, and burnt-on food from pots, pans, and other kitchen surfaces. It delivers powerful cleaning performance without the need for harsh chemicals or excessive scrubbing effort.

The ergonomic design with curved edges gives you a better grip, keeping your fingers and fingernails safe during heavy-duty scrubbing. This makes it suitable for even the most demanding tasks, from cleaning cast-iron cookware to tackling grill stains.

Each box comes with 16 sponges, ensuring you have a steady supply of durable scrubbing tools. Plus, the packaging is eco-friendly and designed with no individual wrap, making it a sustainable choice for your kitchen and the environment.

Best For

This sponge is especially useful for those who want a reliable, long-lasting scrubbing tool. Whether you're preparing for a family dinner, cleaning after a barbecue, or maintaining kitchen equipment, the Scotch-Brite scrub sponge is the perfect companion.

For delicate surfaces like non-stick or glass, we recommend our Scotch-Brite Delicate Scrub Sponge instead. This product is built to help you save time and effort while delivering excellent results.
